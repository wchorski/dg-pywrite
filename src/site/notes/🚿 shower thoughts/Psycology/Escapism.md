## In response to the "Keep politics out of music" persons

There is a lot of confusion when people throw around the word "Escapism". This DOES NOT mean escaping from critical thought. It means to take part in a small community and believe that the rest of the world could be just like this. Mentally ESCAPING a hostile environment for one that coincides with your values and beliefs
